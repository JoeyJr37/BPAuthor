Who I am
Writer, Speaker, Teacher

Benetta Perry has been writing children's stories and poetry for over 40 years. As a well-seasoned follower of Christ, she has taught Bible studies, led Christian youth groups, organized women's ministries and counseled couples. After retiring from 30 years of IBM, she decided to add Christian author to her resume. A native and resident of the deep south, you will feel at home with her writing style.

What I write
From His Word to My Heart: Discovering the Will of God

This Christian journey, called life, is not meant to be traveled alone. Join with the author and walk through these biblical principles together. At some point, on the journey, we all want to know God's will for our lives. As Benetta did, we want to walk in our purpose and grow towards our divine destiny. It is very challenging for many, to clearly discover their purpose. This personal study guide will give you specific biblical principles that are God's will for your life. No matter your spiritual maturity, start today, start here, start now, and allow this personal study guide to help you to level up. Following these biblical principles can give you confidence that your life will be aligned with God's will. After consistently following the action items as outlined in this personal study guide, you can feel confident that you now know the will of God for your life and you are walking, talking, thinking, and living in his will. Today, take this first step and seek to know the will of God for your life.
